About Us

At Auto Cool, we're dedicated to delivering efficient, durable, and custom-tailored air conditioning and refrigeration systems for vehicles and mobile spaces across the Western Cape.

With years of hands-on experience in vehicle climate control, our team understands the unique demands of trucks, buses, RVs, and commercial vans. We don't just install systems — we engineer comfort and reliability for life on the move.

Our technicians have over 15+ years installing and servicing mobile cooling systems — from rooftop units to full refrigerated van setups.
